Introduction

Australia is formally defined by more than 2000 ”Statistical Area Level 2” (SA2) distinct geographical regions, designed to represent communities of between 3000-25000 people ”that interact together socially and economically”. In this assignment, we’ll focus on the 350+ SA2s within the Greater Sydney area.
